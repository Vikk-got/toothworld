# WhatsApp Notifications - Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Get Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Book a Test Appointment
1. Go to your website's booking page
2. Fill in the form with:
   - Your name
   - Your email
   - **Your phone number** (must have WhatsApp)
   - Select dentist, date, time, and service
3. Submit the appointment

### Step 2: Login to Admin Dashboard
1. Navigate to `/admin-login`
2. Enter admin credentials
3. Click on "Appointments" in the sidebar

### Step 3: Accept or Decline
1. Find your test appointment (status: pending)
2. Click the **✓ green button** to accept
   - OR click the **✗ red button** to decline
3. WhatsApp Web opens automatically
4. Click "Send" to deliver the message
5. Check your phone for the notification!

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting

**WhatsApp doesn't open?**
→ Allow popups in your browser

**Message not pre-filled?**
→ Check browser console for errors

**Patient didn't receive message?**
→ Make sure you clicked "Send" in WhatsApp Web

**Phone number format issues?**
→ Try: +919876543210 or 9876543210
